About Us

Founded in 2017, Gelanshow Textiles is a multinational textile enterprise that is well respected among leaders in the market.Supported and approved by the relevant government and state departments across Asia, for our quality and business proficiency, we not only have the mandate to export our goods globally, we are revered for it. With an inherent focus on developing and supplying the finest quality fabrics globally, our company boasts hundreds of looms and over 10,000 square metres of factory space, as well as a fully-automated third- generation modern production line.
